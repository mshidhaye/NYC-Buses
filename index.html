Introduction

As an Analytics onsultant, explored NYC buses data to determine common delay or breakdown reasons, delay times as per companies as well as explored the correlation between specific days of the week and the frequency of breakdown or delays.

Business Questions

  1. What are the common Delay Reasons?
  2. How do delay times vary by bus company and borough?
  3. WIs there a correlation between specific days of the week and the frequency of breakdowns or delays?

About the Data

NYC Buses data for all boroughs was analyzed for years 2019-2023.
